Welcome to the EmotiGame Study

One challenge in understanding skill in competitive gaming is what factors contribute to players skill level. Is it possible to identify patterns in player behavior that reveal skill and cognitive load? EmotiGame is a user research study exploring how biometric data can reveal patterns in gaming skill levels. Our user study brings together 12 participants, 6 skilled and 6 unskilled, to compete in 1v1 League of Legends matches against an expert control player.

As participants play, we track heart rate, keyboard activity, and mouse movement to analyze physiological and behavioral differences between skill levels. Using machine learning, we aim to uncover biometric indicators that may distinguish experienced players from beginners.

This website serves as a hub for participants to log in, complete surveys about their gameplay experience, and stay updated on our findings. Stay tuned as we dive into the science of gaming performance!
